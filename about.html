About Me


Quick introduction: Hi, my name is Norah, I'm sixteen, I live in Lincoln, Nebraska, and I'm graduating a year early.


For undergrad, I'm planning on majoring in either Creative Writing or English. Past that, I'd like to get into a master's program for archiving or librarianship, but this plan may change.


Most of my creative writing focuses on exploring queerness and queer relationships with an emphasis on gender presentation and amatonormativity. I'll also often write about politics, social justice, and family dynamics.
