About Me


Quick introduction: Hi, my name is Norah, I'm sixteen, I live in Lincoln, Nebraska, and I'm graduating a year early. Because I skipped a year of math in middle school and took accelerated French and science classes in freshman and sophomore year, I ended up with just enough credits to graduate this year! Yay!


For undergrad, I'm planning on majoring in either Creative Writing or English. Past that, I'd like to get into a master's program for archiving or librarianship, but we'll see!


Most of my creative writing focuses on exploring queerness and queer relationships with an emphasis on gender presentation and amatonormativity. I also often write about politics, social justice, and family dynamics.
